Recommendation

Option A — Bracketed page

A wins because it tells the function story (document + scanning) without leaning on a camera lens — every competitor uses a lens. The mint corner brackets are a quiet visual signature that survives at 20 px. The forest-green field separates us from the saturated blue/red category. Option B reads as "verified", but the disc dominates and risks looking like a generic "approved" badge. Option C is the most editorial, but the page-stack is fragile at small sizes.

Pair with launch screen A · Minimal light: same tone as the home/library, no jarring brand-flash, ships in < 500 ms.
