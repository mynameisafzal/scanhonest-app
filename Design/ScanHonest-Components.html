Accessibility

required
Dynamic Type — every text style scales up to AX5; layouts reflow vertically (e.g. paywall pricing cards stack).
Contrast — body text ≥ 4.5:1, large text ≥ 3:1. Muted text on white passes at 13px+.
Hit targets — minimum 44 × 44 pt, including the close button on the paywall.
VoiceOver labels — counter announces "Three of five free scans used. Two remaining. Resets May first."
Reduce Motion — pulses become cross-fades; the camera-bracket pulse becomes a static green frame.
Reduce Transparency — translucent camera bar becomes solid rgba(0,0,0,0.85).
Haptics — medium impact on shutter; selection feedback on counter state shift; success notification on Pro purchase.
Color is never the only signal — counter uses both numerals (3 / 5) and the bar gradient.

Anti-pattern checklist

never ship
NEVER
✗ Paywall before first scan
✗ Countdown timers on pricing
✗ Hidden / 6-px close buttons
✗ Dark high-pressure modal
✗ Grey-on-grey footnote disclaimers
✗ Buried Restore Purchase
✗ Surprise scan-limit walls
✗ Fake testimonial names
✗ "99% upgrade" stats
ALWAYS
✓ Both prices shown equal weight
✓ Counter visible from launch
✓ Permission rationale before iOS prompt
✓ Receipt + email post-purchase
✓ "Cancel anytime" as body text
✓ Restore Purchase in Settings & paywall
✓ On-device processing claim provable
✓ Reset date shown literally (May 1)
✓ Plain-English copy throughout
